Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 8, 2026

Welcome to RezuTrack. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

To provide our resume tracking and analysis services, we may collect the following:

  • Documents: PDF files you upload for resume tracking and extraction.
  • Device Info: Basic information about your device and app performance logs.
  • Usage Data: How you interact with the app features.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information exclusively to:

  • Extract and process information from your resumes.
  • Track your job applications and progress.
  • Improve the app's functionality and performance.
  • Process subscriptions via RevenueCat (if applicable).

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information and documents from unauthorized access.

4. Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party services to power RezuTrack:

  • Supabase: For database and file storage.
  • RevenueCat: For managing subscriptions.
  • Vercel: For hosting our backend API services.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your data at any time through the app settings. If you delete your account, all your documents and data will be permanently removed from our servers.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@rezutrack.com.
